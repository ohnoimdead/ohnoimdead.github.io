Golden Oracle
Started: 01.02.2018
Published/Released: 13.12.2018

The Golden Oracle is a book and deck of cards for use in magic and cartomancy. The book and cards are available as a free download from The Golden Oracle and released under the Creative Commons license.

There was a very small initial print run of the cards which are in the hands of a few enthusiasts, however, I’m currently working on a second larger print run which will include a premium print of the full-sized cards, a custom rigid box, and an extended version of the second chapter from the book, which contains the card descriptions. Sale price and launch date are TBD.
